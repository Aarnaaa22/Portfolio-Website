About Me

Hello! I'm RNA, an ambivert who thrives between social gatherings and quiet moments of reflection. I find balance in embracing both sides of my personality, whether I'm out dancing to my favorite tunes or enjoying a peaceful evening at home. Dancing is a passion that brings joy and energy into my life, and there's nothing quite like losing myself in the rhythm of music.

When I'm not dancing, you’ll likely find me listening to music – it’s my go-to escape, a way to express emotions and set the mood for my day. I also dedicate part of my free time to helping stray animals. Feeding and taking care of stray cats and dogs is one of the ways I try to make a positive impact in my community.

This website is an extension of my journey – a space where I share bits and pieces of my life, my passions, and the things that bring me joy. I'm always open to new experiences and meeting like-minded people who share similar interests.
